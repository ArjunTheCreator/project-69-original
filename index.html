campus life

school church

was established in 1978

This school church is an important pillar for the school. This school church helped our students get closer to god and will for years to come. Many people ignore their religion and one of the reasons are studies. But not here. We believe our religion comes first. So, If a prayer is due we will ensure we pray no matter what. And this church is proof of that.



school library

was established in 1980

School library is an important addition for many schools. This school is no different. We strongly believe reading improves vocabulary, speaking skills, understanding of languages, knowledge, etc. Our school library consists of about 50,000+ books on a large variety of topics, genres, etc.

The school auditorium is a great place to showcase our students' talents. The auditorium (since its indoors) made sure that despite of whatever harsh conditions which make it pretty painful outside, The morning assembly, annual day celebration, etc. are still possible.
